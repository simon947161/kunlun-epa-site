# Kunlun EPA Site

Kunlun EPA Site is a public-facing repository for ecological planning, dryland resilience and green value-chain development.

The project focuses on the relationship between fragile landscapes, regional development, ecological protection and long-term sustainability. It is connected to broader work on dryland civilization, ClimateOS and environmental governance.

## Purpose

This repository may support:

- Public website content
- Ecological planning materials
- Green value-chain concepts
- Dryland and desert-edge resilience studies
- Regional development narratives
- Environmental cooperation documents
- Knowledge sharing for future partners

## Why Kunlun matters

The Kunlun region represents more than a geographic area. It is a symbolic frontier between mountains, deserts, water systems, ecological limits and human ambition.

A serious sustainability project in such a region must respect both tradition and future technology. It must combine ecological restoration, local livelihoods, green finance, data systems and long-term governance.
